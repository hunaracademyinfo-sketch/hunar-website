Questions

Answered honestly.

What is Hunar?
Hunar is a Pakistan-based skill academy for students aged 16–22. We help you discover which skill to learn — then teach it in Urdu, with a real instructor available every step of the way. Lessons are delivered one per day via WhatsApp.
Who are these courses for?
Pakistani students aged 16–22 — in school, university, or recently graduated. No prior experience needed. If you can show up for one lesson a day, Hunar takes care of the rest.
Are courses live or recorded?
Both. Pre-recorded lessons come to your WhatsApp daily at a steady, manageable pace. Your instructor is also available live throughout the course for questions and support. You get the structure of recorded content with the support of a live teacher.
How exactly are lessons delivered?
Directly to your WhatsApp — one lesson every morning. No overwhelming Google Drive folders. Just one focused lesson each day that builds on the last. 20–40 minutes, fits any schedule.
How do I pay?
Transfer the course fee to IBAN PK11TMFB0000000073405665 (Tabi Nah Sadaqat — Easypaisa Digital Bank). Then click "Enroll Now", fill your details, upload your payment screenshot and transaction ID. Our team verifies within 24 hours and your instructor adds you to the course group.
Can I contact my instructor directly?
Yes — directly. Ayesha, Sadia, and Sabawoon are available within their course groups to answer questions, review work, and re-explain anything you don't understand. This is one of the core things that makes Hunar different.
I don't know which skill to choose — what do I do?
That's exactly why we created the free orientation session. Enter your WhatsApp number at the top of this page — we'll send you a video that walks you through how to choose the right skill for your situation. No cost, no commitment.
What is your refund policy?
If you're not satisfied within the first 3 days, contact us on WhatsApp and we'll work out a resolution. We'd rather help you find the right course than keep your money for the wrong one.
